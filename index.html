Petronella is an interactive story set across Eindhoven, created for local residents. Journey into the past and follow the story of Petronella, along with another city explorer, a person you might or might not have seen around before. The development of this interactive story was made possible by the stadsmakers community fonds.

This interactive story is developed by studio vanpauli. You can contact me at studiovanpauli@proton.me.

How do I participate?
Sign up via the button in the banner, you are asked for your availabilities during an average week. Once someone else signs up with similar availabilities you will be sent further instructions.

Is it free?
Yes, Petronella is a free experience open to everyone.

Can I play alone?
No, the story is made to be experienced in groups of two. You will be matched with someone else that has also signed up.

What if I don't want to play with a stranger?
In the case that you would like to play it with your family, or in the case that you do not live in Eindhoven you can request to play the game with the person/people of your choice.

How long does it take?
You can go through the story at your own pace. Most participants spend around two hours to complete the story.

How accessible is the story?
Participants move through the city on foot. All locations are wheelchair accessible, and no special physical movements are required. The story format on the phone is screen reader compatible, but physical props do not include additional support for those with visual impairments.

Do I need anything special?
No, you only need a phone with internet to download the initial story file at the beginning.
